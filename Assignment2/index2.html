Mt. Elbert

Mount Elbert is the highest summit of the Rocky Mountains of North America and the highest point in the U.S. state of Colorado and the entire Mississippi River drainage basin. Wikipedia

Elevation: 14,439′
Prominence: 9,072′

Location: High point of Lake County and the State of Colorado, U.S.
